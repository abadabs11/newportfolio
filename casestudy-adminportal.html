Dashboard 1.0: The Good
Current branding capabilities are world-class Our mobile platform is white-labeled for 600+ gyms. Take a second to imagine the ugliest brand you can think of. Brown on brown? Stripes on plaid? Yeah, it needs to work with that. And we’ve built a platform that can accommodate even the ugliest brand in the blink of an eye.
We have a wide breadth of features & integrations With over 30 configurable features, including add-ons, a club can easily customize their experience to fit their member base. With integrations that allow a user to quickly check-in to the club & connect their favorite fitness device, our mobile platform acts as the hub of the health club industry.
